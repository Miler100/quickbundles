General Questions

Find solutions to the most common questions we receive.

How fast will I receive my bundle or checker?

Delivery is instant once payment is confirmed. Checkers are sent immediately, and bundles are loaded within minutes.

Do you accept only MTN MoMo?

Yes. We only accept MTN MoMo at the moment. Telecel and AirtelTigo payments are not supported.

What details do I need to buy a checker?

We only need the candidate’s phone number and exam year. Additional details (name/index) help speed up the process.

How do I track my order?

Visit the Track Order page and enter your order ID or phone number.

Can I buy for someone else?

Yes. Just enter their phone number during checkout.

Do you offer refunds?

If a service is not delivered, you will receive a full refund. Delivered checkers or bundles cannot be refunded.
